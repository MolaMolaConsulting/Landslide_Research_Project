About Us

Welcome to Mola Mola Consulting Services.

We are a specialty consulting firm focused on utilizing Geographic Information Science (GIS) to accurately identify geographical areas susceptible to landslides.

Our GIS capabilities and modelling products enable our clients to formulate comprehensive and effective management plans to address emergency response management, sustainability and resilience, planning, zoning, building code requirements, risk assessment, property value and environmental conservation.
